Scholarship Detail

A scholarship visa is a ticket to international studies or education. A person applying for it may know that scholarship visa is a non-immigrant visa. You apply for your favorite program there and get a chance to visit the country for educational purposes. The only difference Scholarship visa makes from Study visa is that scholarship visa’s are fully funded and you are not asked to pay for your education. Some countries have a certain percentage for the scholarship they are offering and some are providing fully sponsored scholarships. However, foreign education acts like a magnet to students who are enthusiastically willing to learn.
We will be directing you via links to how to apply for scholarships from original sources. As our website’s kept up-to-dated so you’ll be easily getting updates regarding any scholarship that is being offered from any country or university. While applying for scholarship a person must know followings:
• Is he/she eligible for that certain scholarship?
• Do you meet their required grades for scholarship?
• Is that scholarship still available for your required course?
• You must have passed their native’s language course or at least should be on your list.
There are a lot of scholarships available on our site. Have a check!
Note: Every visa requirement is different for different country.
